A while passed as Vaguely gathered the energy to pry the block out, seemingly for no reason other than to satiate a morbid curiosity.
After heaving it out, she lay back, exhausted.
"Hello?!"
"Wait what was that?" Vaguely thought, slightly stunned.
"Hello?"
A cry, a call!
Could someone be reaching out to Vaguely?

Frightened and covering her ears, Vaguely decided to sit in despair, hoping that the strange voice would go away.
